A Blog is Born

Hello! I've been wanting to say this for a while, and I finally can...

Welcome to my Blog!!!

Making something like this was always a huge goal of the website, and It's nice to finally have done it.
I feel the need to almost introduce myself again, but I think that's already been done through my about me section... Though that page still needs some more work, if I'm being honest.

I feel like there are so many things I want to discuss and just let my mind run loose about; future plans for the site, projects I'm working on, and my goals in computer science both academically and professionally!

I've done my best to separate everything across their own pages, with everything being accessible through the home page. I know a lot of other small sites will put everything on one page to remain a static site, but I really enjoy a menu layout, I guess. It adds more clicks for sure, and I'm 100% the type of person to optimize that sort of thing down to 0 clicks... but I'm almost scared that if I try to I'll loose the feel I'm going for with my 'Windows XP Theme', or at the very least severely complicate it. So yeah. For the time being, the menu layout stays.

At the moment of writing this, the blog site is pretty bare bones. While the rest of my site is fairly fleshed out with features and styling, this was made in a couple hours. I'm hoping in the future to add a tagging system and an easier search function for when this blog gets more posts.

I'm really hoping to make use of this blog more, so feel free to sign up for the RSS feed! It's linked on the right hand column.

-Ethan
