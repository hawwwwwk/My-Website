FAQ (probably)

Q: What's with the background?
A: So. I'm a huge fan of the original Windows XP Bliss wallpaper, and I wanted to make a version of it that was a little more... dynamic; and something that was my own, of course. So I made a day and night version of it! The shot was captured in Blender and then dithered to look grainy and have less colors overall. The movement was made by cycling between two layers of dithering.
