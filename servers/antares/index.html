Antares

Antares is my newest server! It runs my Unraid NAS, and a few other things.

Specs

  • Model: Dell PowerEdge R710
  • CPU: 2x Intel Xeon L5630 SLBVD @ 2.13GHz
  • RAM: 96GB DDR3 ECC
  • Storage: 6x 4TB 7.2K SAS, 1x 1TB SATA SSD
  • OS: Unraid OS

Services

  • Just Nextcloud at the moment!

Notes

  • Antares is named after the star Antares, the brightest star in the constellation Scorpius.
  • Antares is the reason I made my naming scheme stars! I love astronomy, and I though would be a cute way to show that.
  • Loaded with a total 24TB of HDD storage, with 2 of the drives being parity and a 1TB drive being cache. 16TB of usable storage isn't terrible!
